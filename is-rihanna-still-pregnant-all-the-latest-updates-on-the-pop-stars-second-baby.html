Rihanna Net Worth

Rihanna is now worth $1.7 billion, making her the richest female musician in the world and the second-richest female entertainer after Oprah Winfrey.

The bulk of her wealth comes from Fenty Beauty, which she co-owns with French luxury giant LVMH.

The cosmetics company, which launched in 2017 with the goal of inclusivity, has been a huge hit among consumers of all skin tones and backgrounds.

Forbes estimates that Fenty Beauty is worth $2.8 billion, and Rihanna owns 50% of it.

Rihanna also owns 30% of Savage x Fenty, her lingerie line that raised money at a $1 billion valuation in February 2021.

The brand is known for its diverse and body-positive campaigns and shows. Forbes estimates that Rihanna’s stake in Savage x Fenty is worth $270 million.

Rihanna’s music and acting career also contribute to her fortune, although not as much as her businesses.

She has sold over 250 million records worldwide, making her one of the best-selling artists of all time.

Rihanna has won nine Grammy Awards, 13 American Music Awards, and 12 Billboard Music Awards, among other honors.

She has also starred in films like Battleship, Home, Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets, and Ocean’s 8.

Rihanna Age

Rihanna was born on February 20, 1988 in Saint Michael, Barbados.

She is currently 35 years old.

Rihanna Parents

Rihanna’s parents are Ronald Fenty and Monica Braithwaite.

Her father is of Barbadian and Irish descent, while her mother is of Afro-Guyanese ancestry.

Rihanna has two brothers, Rorrey and Rajad Fenty, and three half-siblings from her father’s side: Kandy, Samantha, and Jamie Fenty.
